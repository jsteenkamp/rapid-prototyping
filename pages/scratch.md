import SuperBox from 'superbox';

# Start with a Box

[SuperBox](https://github.com/jxnblk/superbox) a primitive component for your styles from [Brent Jackson](https://twitter.com/jxnblk).

<SuperBox>
  SuperBox
</SuperBox>

Our SuperBox code:

```html
<SuperBox
    fontSize={[0, 8, 12]}
    m={6}
    p={4}
    color='white'
    bg='cornflowerblue'>
  SuperBox
</SuperBox>
```
